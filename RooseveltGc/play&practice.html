Play & practice

Roosevelt GC

Roosevelt Golf Club is a private indoor Trackman trainer and simulator with a limited membership. Keep your game in shape all winter with practice or, play Pebble Beach, The Old Course or any of numerous Trackman golf courses. New courses added regularly. There are challenge par 3s, closest to the pin contests or an entertaining range with lots of targets.
